About

Hi, I'm Tim.

TM

I'm a commercial founder and operator working across energy, EVs and carbon. I live in London, and most of what I do sits somewhere between sustainability, energy markets, and getting useful things into the world.

I studied Geography at Edinburgh — wrote my dissertation on community-owned renewable micro-grids — and spent a year afterwards doing a permaculture course in Bristol. Since then I've been building and joining early-stage businesses in the energy transition.

I was an early hire at Abatable, a carbon finance platform. After that I co-founded Ecofit Homes, a UK retrofit data business that was acquired by Utilita Energy in 2025. Most recently I spent a year at Octopus Electric Vehicles working on cross-business strategy. These days I'm working independently — commercial and strategy work for seed-stage energy and cleantech businesses.
